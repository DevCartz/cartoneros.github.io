About Us

Welcome to SafeZone: Disasters Alert – Be Informed, Be Prepared, your trusted online platform for disaster awareness, preparedness, and real-time updates. Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and communities in the Philippines with life-saving information and practical resources to stay safe before, during, and after disasters. As a public service initiative, SafeZone was developed to address the growing need for accessible and reliable disaster-related information in a user-friendly and responsive website. Whether it's a typhoon, earthquake, flood, or fire, we aim to help people make informed decisions through verified alerts, safety guides, and community support features. Our website offers: Real-time updates from official government sources (PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NDRRMC) Step-by-step preparedness guides and emergency checklists Educational content such as articles, videos, and infographics Reporting tools to raise awareness of local incidents Emergency hotline directory and downloadable resources We believe that awareness saves lives. Through technology and community engagement, SafeZone serves as a digital hub for learning, sharing, and acting responsibly during disasters.

Reliable Alerts


Get real-time alerts on typhoons, earthquakes, and other disasters from trusted government sources like PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, and NDRRMC. Stay one step ahead and make timely decisions to protect yourself and your family.

Preparedness Tools


Access easy-to-follow emergency plans, checklists, and tips tailored for families, schools, and communities. SafeZone provides practical resources to help you prepare for any disaster—before it strikes.

Community Engagement


Connect, share, and support one another. Report local incidents, access educational content, and use our interactive features to help build a safer, more resilient community across the Philippines.
