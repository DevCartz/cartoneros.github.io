✅ Be Prepared: Disaster Preparedness Tips





Your safety begins with preparation. Follow these essential tips to get ready:

📝 General Preparedness Checklist:

  • 📦 Create an Emergency Kit (water, food, flashlight, first-aid, etc.)
  • 📞 Know Emergency Contacts (BFP, PNP, LGU hotlines)
  • 🏠 Make a Family Disaster Plan
  • 📍 Know Your Area’s Risks (flood, earthquake, etc.)
  • 🧠 Stay Informed via NDRRMC, PHIVOLCS, PAGASA

🧰 Additional Tips:

  • Teach children emergency calls
  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances
  • Keep a Go-Bag ready for each family member
