Flood Information

Understand floods, their causes, effects, and safety tips.

What is a Flood?

A flood is an overflow of water that submerges land which is usually dry. It can result from rain, river overflow, dam breakage, or melting snow.

Causes of Floods

  • Heavy rainfall
  • River overflow
  • Dam failure
  • Urban drainage issues
  • Melting snow or ice

Effects of Floods

  • Damage to infrastructure
  • Loss of life and property
  • Waterborne diseases
  • Displacement of people

Flood Safety Tips

  1. Move to higher ground immediately.
  2. Do not walk or drive through floodwaters.
  3. Stay informed via weather alerts.
  4. Have an emergency kit ready.
  5. Listen to emergency officials and follow instructions.
